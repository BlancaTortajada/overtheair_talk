How we started

  • Hack so we could talk to friends on Slack
  • No IU or support
  • Still people used it!

Since we launched

  • Launched unintentionally in 2014
  • Clients by word of mouth
  • Started working full-time

Downs

  • Product with a young API
  • Difficulty providing a good experience
  • Uncertainty on partnerships

Ups

  • Discoverability increases thanks to Slack
  • Increasing potential user base
  • Using your own product to talk to your users

Lessons learned

  • Listen to your users and talk to them
  • Launch early but give good support
  • Be open with Slack from day 1

What's next?

  • More flexible API for better experience?
  • New products around Slack: Roomino, NestorHQ, Tatsu…
  • New playground to solve problems around collaboration
