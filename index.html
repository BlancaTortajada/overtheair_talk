Slackline

Opportunities & Challenges

Reach

  • + Slack is getting a lot of attention
  • + Slack customers are early-adopters
  • − Harder to get access to Slack's team
  • − Harder to get coverage with so many things being built

User Experience

  • + Building around a tool used by thousands and with high engagement
  • + Huge potential for solving new problems
  • − Sometimes setup can take time and effort

API

  • + Very rich and flexible
  • − Not consistent or well-documented sometimes

Tips

  • Keep branding in mind (Slack Chats, Slack Features, etc.)
  • Be open with Slack
  • Chat with us!

    https://slackline.io/shared_channels/slack-api

Conclusion

  • Huge potential
  • Some challenges
  • Still room for new products
